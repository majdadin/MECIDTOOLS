# Privacy Policy for mecidtools

Last updated: July 2026

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application MecidTools ("Application") created by majdaddin with help of Ai. The Application is an open-source add-on designed to extend the functionality of Autodesk® software.

### 1. Information Collection and Use
* **Personal Data:** The Application does not collect, harvest, or request any personally identifiable information (PII) from users, such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or physical locations.
* **Usage Data:** The Application operates entirely locally on your machine. It does not track your commands, usage statistics, design file details, or application history.
* **Network Traffic:** The Application does not connect to the internet, transmit any data to external servers, or use telemetry tracking software.

### 2. Third-Party Access
Because the Application does not collect or store any information, no data is ever sold, traded, or shared with third-party individuals, advertising networks, or analytics companies.

### 3. Open Source Transparency
The entire source code for this Application is publicly available for auditing at https://github.com/majdadin/MECIDTOOLS/tree/main. Users are welcome to review the codebase to verify its data privacy compliance.

### 4. Changes to this Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time for any reason. We will notify you of any changes by updating this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top.
